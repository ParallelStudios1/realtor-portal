## 1. Who we are

Realtor Portal ("we", "us", "our") is operated by [Parallel Studios LLC] (the "Company"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Realtor Portal mobile app (iOS and Android) and admin panel (the "Service").

## 2. Information we collect

When you use the Service, we collect:

- **Account information**: name, email address, phone number, role (realtor or client), and the firm you belong to.
- **Profile information**: optional avatar, optional contact info you choose to enter.
- **Deal information**: client search names, house addresses, important dates (closing, inspection, etc.), uploaded PDF documents, photos, messages between you and your realtor or client.
- **Device information**: push notification token, device platform (iOS or Android), app version.
- **Usage information**: pages viewed, actions taken, timestamps. We use this to fix bugs and improve the Service.

We do NOT collect: your location, your contacts, your photo library beyond files you explicitly upload, or financial / banking information.

## 3. How we use your information

- To provide the Service: show you your deal, send you notifications, route messages between realtors and clients.
- To support you: respond to questions or troubleshooting requests.
- To improve the Service: identify and fix bugs, prioritize features.
- To bill firms: licensing fees are charged to firms (not individuals); we share aggregate usage data with billing providers (Stripe).

We do NOT sell your information. We do NOT share your information with advertisers.

## 4. Who we share with

We share information only with:

- **Your firm.** Realtors at your firm can see your account, your deal, and any messages or documents shared with them. This is the entire point of the Service.
- **Service providers** that help us operate the Service: Supabase (database and storage), Expo (push notifications), Stripe (billing for firms), Vercel (admin panel hosting). Each has its own privacy practices.
- **Legal requirements.** If we are required by law (court order, subpoena, etc.) to disclose information, we will.

## 5. Data retention

- **Active accounts**: we keep your data as long as your firm has an active subscription with us.
- **After firm cancels**: we keep deal data for 90 days, then delete. Firms can request earlier deletion via written request.
- **Account deletion**: you can request deletion of your personal account by emailing privacy@yourdomain.com. We will delete your record within 30 days, except where a firm has a legal need to retain transaction records.

## 6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal information.
- Correct inaccurate information.
- Delete your information.
- Object to or restrict processing.
- Data portability.

Email privacy@yourdomain.com to exercise any of these rights.

## 7. Children

The Service is intended for adults (18+) acting as real estate professionals or clients. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard technical and administrative measures: encrypted database connections (TLS), encrypted at-rest storage, role-based access controls, and Postgres row-level security to ensure firms cannot see each other's data. No system is perfectly secure; you use the Service at your own risk.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you in the app and update the "Last updated" date above.
